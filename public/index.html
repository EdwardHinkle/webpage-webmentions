A WebMention Endpoint

Easily accept and embed WebMentions on all type of sites

How do I receive?

You receive WebMentions through this service in three easy steps:

  1. You sign in using one of these services:

  2. Then you add your the domain name (eg. example.com) of the site you want to accept WebMentions for.

  3. Lastly you add the WebMention endpoint to the HTML-head of your posts to accept mentions and a HTML-snippet where you want to embed them:

  4. (And after you've done all that feel free to add a final touch with some nice styles)

How do I send?

For now refer to the IndieWebCamp wiki for information on how to send WebMentions.
